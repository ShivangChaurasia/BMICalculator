BMI Categories

Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
Normal weight: BMI 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight: BMI 25 - 29.9
Obese: BMI 30 or greater

Tips for Healthy BMI

Balanced Diet

Eat a variety of nutrient-rich foods in appropriate portions.

Regular Exercise

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity per week.

Adequate Sleep

Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night for optimal health.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day for metabolism support.
